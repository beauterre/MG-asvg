This is an example of an animation exported directly from Adobe Animate.
I exported a movie as files and got 132 exported consecutive SVG files from Adobe Animate (not optimized for character animator, just SVG) these I dragged into the converter and that spat out this ASVG, then I just placed it in a demo.
The converter is free as well, check the readme!


One CaveAt: The tweening is not supported out of the box. So break any tweens into keyframes and break your shapes into symbols and you will be fine!

133 SVG of 18Kb (2.3 Mb) => ASVG=560Kb, 2000x2000 APNG=8.0MB, 2000x2000 GIF (32color)=6.7MB
