This is an example of an animation exported directly from Adobe Animate.
I exported a movie as files and got 132 exported consecutive SVG files from Adobe Animate (not optimized for character animator, just SVG) these I dragged into the converter and that spat out this ASVG after some cruching, then I just placed it in a demo.
The converter is free as well, check the readme!


One CaveAt: shape tweening is not supported by the export. So break your shape tweened frames into keyframes and you will be fine!
